About

Cupcakery Diva is located in downtown New York City and specializes in outside the box, couture cupcakes. Each order is artistically tailored, both in taste and design, to the theme of any event - intimate or outrageous.

Truly the "icing on the cake," these homemade sweet (or savory) treats will impress your guests, enhance the overall atmosphere, and create a fun, fabulous, unforgettable experience.

And because you are never fully dressed or party ready without some flare, every cupcake is decked out in a colorful glitter skirt.

Sure, we have the basics: Vanilla, Chocolate, Peanut Butter, Cookies & Cream, Red Velvet, etc. But what are you really craving?

Our cakes are custom made and designed with theme in mind; Birthdays, Beach Parties, Christenings, and even Work Events

And be sure to ask about our mini's and specialty cakes!
